Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Frisco services and resources

Most community programs can be registered through the Play Frisco website at playfrisco.org or by visiting the Recreation Center in person. Some programs require advance registration, while others offer walk-in enrollment. Contact us at 972-292-5000 for specific program details.

The Frisco Public Library is typically open Monday through Saturday from 9 AM to 6 PM, and Sunday from 1 PM to 5 PM. Hours may vary by location and holiday schedules. Visit friscolibrary.com for specific branch hours and holiday closures.

Business licenses and permits can be obtained through the Development Services Department. Visit the City of Frisco website at friscotexas.gov or call 972-292-5131 for specific requirements, fees, and application procedures.

Always call 911 for emergencies. For non-emergency police calls, contact the Frisco Police Department at 972-292-5020. For fire-related questions and non-emergency assistance, call Frisco Fire at 972-292-6000.

Report infrastructure issues such as potholes, street lights, or damaged sidewalks through the City of Frisco website at friscotexas.gov or by calling 972-292-5000. You can also submit a service request online through the citizen portal.

Yes, Frisco has numerous parks and recreation facilities. Play Frisco manages these facilities and offers a variety of programs. Visit playfrisco.org for information about parks, sports leagues, fitness classes, and community activities available throughout the city.
